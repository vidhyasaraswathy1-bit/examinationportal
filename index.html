Online Examination Instructions

General Guidelines

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection throughout the exam.
  • Find a quiet and well-lit space to take the test without distractions.
  • Keep your device fully charged or connected to a power source.
  • Read all instructions carefully before starting the exam.

Timing Rules

  • The exam has a fixed duration. The timer will start as soon as you click "Start Test".
  • The "Start Test" button will only be active during the specified exam window.
  • Your test will be automatically submitted when the time expires.

Before You Begin

  • Close all other applications, tabs, and windows on your computer.
  • Have a piece of scratch paper and a pen/pencil ready if allowed.
  • Use a recommended browser (e.g., latest version of Chrome or Firefox).

Prohibited Actions

  • Do not use any external resources like books, notes, or websites unless permitted.
  • Do not communicate with any other person during the exam.
  • Do not attempt to copy, print, or capture screenshots of the exam questions.
  • Do not refresh the page or use the browser's back/forward buttons.

Important Notes

Violation of any of the rules may lead to disqualification. In case of any technical issues, contact the administrator immediately.
